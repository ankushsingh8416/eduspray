IPU: Courses, Fee, Admissions 2024, Cutoff, Ranking, Placements

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), popularly known as IP University or IPU is a state university, located in Delhi. GGSIPU is one of the largest universities in Delhi with a campus area of 78 acres and an enrollment of more than 8000 students. IPU is recognized by the UGC and accredited with an ‘A’ grade by the NAAC. It has been ranked 74th among the universities in India by NIRF 2023. IPU has more than 120 affiliated colleges out of which 83 are self-financed and 35 are owned and managed by the Government of NCT of Delhi. IPU offers over 50 UG, PG, and Research programs in the fields of Arts, Science, Commerce, Engineering, Management, Law, and Medicine among others. The university is quite popular for its BTech, MBA, BBA, BCA, and LLB programs. Admissions at IPU for most of the courses are conducted through IPU CET (Common Entrance Test) followed by a counselling process. The university also accepts scores of national-level tests such as CUET, JEE Main, GATE, NIMCET, CAT, and NEET for admission to various courses. Check IPU Admission 2024 GGSIPU placement rate is more than 90%. During the placement drive of 2022, the university witnessed an increase of 41% in the highest and average package as compared to the previous year. The highest package offered was INR 45 LPA, while the average package stood at NR 12 LPA. Top recruiters at IPU are Adobe, Oracle, Facebook, Google, Microsoft, etc.

IPU Application Process 2024

Application forms for all the courses can be filled online by visiting the official website of GGSIPU. Follow the given steps in order to apply online.

  • Visit the official website of IP University.
  • Fill the application form by providing General and Professional Information along with Payment details.
  • Upload the scanned copies of desired documents.
  • Verify the details you have provided in the form and check the Declaration box.
  • Submit the application form and note User ID & Password.
  • Pay the application fee (INR 1500) by Credit/ Debit Card or through Net-Banking.
  • After fee submission, course details along with transaction ID will be displayed.
  • Take the printout of this page for further admission requirements.

IPU Result

IPU follows an odd and even semester pattern for all the courses offered. Term-End examinations for even and odd semesters are conducted in the months of May/June and December, respectively. IPU releases results within 1-2 months after successful completion of the exam. Students who are not satisfied with the marks can apply for the revelation of answer scripts by paying a required fee. IPU results can be checked and downloaded by visiting the official website of the university.

How To Check IPU Result?

Students can follow the step-by-step process mentioned below to check and download the IPU result:

  • 1. Log on to the official website of GGSIPU
  • 2. Click on the ‘Examinations’ tab on home page.
  • 3. Click on the ‘Examination Results’ from the drop down menu.
  • 4. Upon clicking the relevant link, the result will automatically download to your device.

IPU FAQs
Ques. How many institutes are affiliated to IPU?

Ans. There are more than 120 institutes which are affiliated to IPU. Some of the important ones include:

  • College of Nursing, VMMC & Safdarjang Hospital
  • Guru Nanak Dev Institute of Technology, Institutional Area, Rohini
  • Bharati Vidyapeeth Institute of Computer Applications & Management
Ques. Does IPU conduct CET for all admissions?

Ans. Yes, majorly all programs accept CET scores for admission to various courses at IPU. This is followed by counselling for final seat allocation.

Ques. Is a physical fitness certificate mandatory for admission at IPU?

Ans. Yes, an applicant’s mental and physical well being is important for his/her admission to avoid hindrances in educational or professional or even extracurricular activities. Thus, the university encourages the students for the same.

Ques. Is the application fee refundable for the CET exam at IPU?

Ans. Any student who backs out at the last minute for appearing for any CET exam would not be refunded the application fee of INR 1200. Requests for the same would not be entertained by IPU.

Ques. Does IPU offer internship and placement opportunities to its students?

Ans. Yes, internships were last offered in 2018 by Paytm. While placements are offered to MBA students post their degree completion.

Ques. Where is IPU situated?

Ans. Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University was established in 1998, and situated at Sector 16 C, Dwarka, 110078, New Delhi. It is an AICTE and UGC approved University.

Do DU Colleges provide any reservation for students who are residents of Delhi?

No, DU colleges do not provide any such reservation to the residents of Delhi.

Ques. Is IPU a state or private University?

Ans. IPU is a state University and was formerly known as Indraprastha University. The University offers several B.Tech, M.tech, B.Arch, MBA, , LLB, B.ed, MBBS courses under various disciplines.

Ques. Is CET score mandatory for IPU admission?

Ans. Yes, CET (Common Entrance Test) score is mandatory for admission to GGSIPU. You have to appear for the CET exam and meet the required cut off marks to get admission in various UG, PG programs.

Ques. What are the facilities offered by IPU to its students?

Ans. IPU offers hostel facilities separately for both boys and girls, library, canteen, medical room, sports ground. Auditorium, seminar room, conference room, parking facilities are also available in the university premises.

Ques. Does IPU offer scholarships to meritorious students?

Ans. Yes. IPU offers scholarships and financial assistance to meritorious students. Some of the scholarships are the Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for college and university students, National Fellowship and Scholarship for Higher Education for ST students, Central Scheme of Scholarship for students with disabilities.

Ques. Who are the top recruiters of IPU?

Ans. Renowned companies such as Infosys, Cognizant, TCS, Google, Wipro visit IPU to hire the best students. Internships are also offered to their students by some companies.

Ques. What is the placement record of IPU?

Ans. An average package of 5 Lakh p.a. was offered to the students of IPU. The highest amount of 16 Lakh p.a. was also bagged by a student from IPU.

Ques. What is the fee for B.Tech program at IPU?

Ans. B.tech students have to pay INR 1.17 Lakhs per annum for admission to IPU. As IPU offers scholarships, meritorious will get discounts on their admission fees.
